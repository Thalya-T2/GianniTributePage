The Life of Gianna Bryant (2006-2020)

Gianna Maria Onore Bryant also referred to as "Gigi", was born on May 1 2006, in Los Angeles California USA, to Vanessa Laine Bryant and Kobe Bryant. She was raised alongside her sisters; Natalia, Bianka, and Capri. Her mother Vanessa worked as a model, while her father Kobe Bryant was a famous basketball player who won five NBA championships with the popular NBA team Los Angeles Lakers. As she grew up watching her father play basketball, Gianna developed an interest in the sport at a young age and wanted to follow in the footsteps of her famous father. Gianna Bryant was enrolled at Harbor Day School, a private school in Newport Beach, California. She started playing competitive basketball at her school and soon became part of the school’s basketball team. She went on to become a popular and one of the most important players for her middle school basketball team

Gianna Bryant was close to her father and enjoyed spending time with him. She often attended WNBA, UConn, and Los Angeles Lakers’ games along with her father.

  • In 2011, she started playing basketball at the Mamba Sports Academy where she received training from her father.
  • In 2020, she started playing for the club Mamba Ballers at the U-10 level and stayed there until she reached the U-15 level
  • In January 26, 2020, Gianna and her father boarded a Sikorsky S-76 helicopter at John Wayne Airport in California and started their journey towards Camarillo Airport to witness a basketball game at the Mamba Academy. Unfortunately, the helicopter crashed into one of the mountains in Calabasas, killing all nine occupants including Kobe and Gianna
  • In February 22, 2020, Kobe and Gianna Bryant receive a tribute memorial at the 51st NAACP Image Awards.
  • In April 17, 2020, Bryant is awarded the Honary Pick of the Women’s NBA draft with Payton Chester and Alyssa Altobelli.
